Fun Facts About Animals

How to make a camel.

  • Camels are domestic animals
  • Camels can go without water for 7 months
  • Camels live in hot locations

How to make a Chameleon.

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • And many species have the ability to change color
  • Most species, the larger ones in particular, also have a prehensile tail

How to make a Pigeon.

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • They are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals

How to make a Teddy Bear.

  • Teddy bears are fluffy,cute and every kid loves paying and huging it :)
  • They are in many colors like red, blue, pink and are with cute messages
  • They may often have human-like features, They are usually small and soft

How to make a Panda.

  • They have great camouflage for their environment
  • Their eyes are different to normal bears
  • They spend a lot of their day eating, Bamboo is critical to their diet

How to make a Cicada.

  • Cicadas are any group of sound-producing insects
  • They prune mature trees, aerate the soil
  • And once they die their bodies serve as an important source of nitrogen for growing trees
